What is AUHack?

AUHack is Aarhus University's largest hackathon. Over a 36-hour period from November 13 to 15, hackers from around the world will gather on campus to create and innovate on their projects. This weekend gives you the opportunity to try out some of your most crasiest ideas, you otherwise never would have the time to complete. If you are up for it you might also learn some new people and even pair up and create even more fantastic hacks!

FAQ

Should I come to this hackathon?

YES of course! If you are a student and above 18 years old. All disciplines are welcome.

Is it for free?

Yes. Thanks to our sponsors we will provide free food, swag, snacks, drinks and a place to sleep. If you come from abroad we will furthermore try to help you with accommodation, transportation and maybe some sightseeing advice.

How can I apply?

You must fill out the application form, after completing the form you will receive an email that confirms you have applied, after this you will receive a confirmations to attend. Confirmation emails will be send on a rolling basis. The applications are open!

When is it?

AUHack will start in the evening of Friday, November 13th November with the opening ceremony. ?There will be planned events events friday for those who arrive early?. All work eligible for prizes must be completed between the opening and closing ceremony. The closing ceremony will start Sunday noon. There will throughout the hackathon be hosted small events and talks. For a more detailed schedule see above.

Do I need to have a team?

No. You are welcome to make a team before the event, but loads of people come without a team. We will help everyone finding a team in the beginning of the event. Your team members can be from other universities around the world. One of the best things about hackathons is meeting hackers from all over the country.

I cannot code. Can I attend anyways?

You should definitely still attend, whether you have written lots of code or not even a single line. Other people have learned to code at hackathons, and we welcome you to try the same. In the hackathon spirit are all students are very friendly and willing to help each other out.

Can I build on my hack before the event?

No. It’s unfair. You can think of ideas but not build them. You can of course use libraries, frameworks, and templates.

What can I win?

You have a fantastic opportunity to learn new stuff and make new friends, isn’t that just great? Everyone will be a winner!... Okay, we will also have some kick-ass prices. They will be revealed later on.

I don’t know anyone else attending. Should I attend on my own?

Don’t let that stop you. A lot of other students will be coming on their own as well. We will have an amazing weekend! We will help everyone finding a team in the beginning of the event. Your team members can be from other universities around the world. One of the best things about hackathons is meeting hackers from all over the country.

How do i get there?

After your registration is confirmed and you receive word that you’re coming to AUHack, we’ll share information about buses and possible travel reimbursements. More than likely, we’ll be sending a bus to a University near you.

What do I need to bring?

Need to have: Laptop and charger, cellphone and charger, sleeping bag, pillow, toiletries, a couple changes of clothes, well-rested and open mind.
Don’t need: a pre-determined team, and idea for an app, food or drink.
Absolutely no need to have: weapons, alcohol, illegal drugs, soldering equipment, animals, bad mood
