What is AUHack?

AUHack is Aarhus University’s largest hackathon. Over a 36-hour period from April 8th to 10th, hackers from around the country will gather on campus to innovate and hack on their projects. AUHack gives you the opportunity to try out what you learn in a practical setting and try out your crazy ideas. If you are up for it, join and get to know new people, maybe even pair up at the hackathon and create fantastic hacks together. Come join us in Aarhus.

FAQ

Should I come to this hackathon?

YES of course! If you are a student and above 18 years old. Students from all study programmes are welcome.

Is it for free?

Yes. Thanks to our sponsors we will provide free food, swag, snacks, drinks and a place for you to sleep at the hackathon.

How can I apply?

You must fill out the application form. After completing the form, we at the AUHack team will send you an email confirming your spots. Confirmation emails will be send out on a rolling basis.

When is it?

AUHack will start in the evening of Friday, April 8th with an opening ceremony. The closing ceremony will start Sunday, April 10th at noon. All work eligible for prizes must be worked on and completed between the ceremonies. Throughout the hackathon there will be hosted small events and relevant talks. For a more detailed schedule see above.

Do I need to have a team?

No. You are more than welcome to make a team before the hackathon, but loads of people come without a team. We will help everyone finding a team friday when people arrive. We recommend groups of 3-4 persons, but anything between 2-5 is allowed.

I cannot code. Can I attend anyway?

Sure, you should definitely attend! Whether you have written lots of code or just have a creative soul, this hackathon needs you! In true hackathon spirit all students are very friendly and willing to help each other out.

Can I build on my hack before the event?

No. Thats not fair. You can come up with ideas, but not build them. You are of course allowed to use libraries, frameworks and templates for your hacks.

What can I win?

You have the fantastic opportunity to learn new stuff and make new friends, isn’t that just great? Everyone will be a winner!… Okay, we will also have some awesome prices at AUHack. They will be revealed at a later date.

Can i sleep at the AUHack venue?

There will be rooms to sleep in, that is if you have time! You will have to bring your own bedroll, sleeping bag and pillows though.

How do I get there?

After your registration is confirmed and you have received word from us that you got a spot at AUHack, we will share travel information on how to arrive at Aarhus University. If you are a large group of students, we will most likely be able to send a bus to your University.
The address is:
Finlandsgade 21, 8200 Aarhus N.

What do I need to bring?

Need to have: Laptop, cellphone and your chargers. A sleeping bag, pillow if you need, toiletries, clothes and a well-rested and open mind. If you want to hack on hardware, you might need to bring it yourself.
You won’t need to have: a pre-determined team, an idea for a hack, or food and drinks. There is absolutely no reason to bring illegal drugs or bad mood.

What if this FAQ didn't answer my questions?

Send an email to: contact@auhack.org
