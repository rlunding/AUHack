What is AUHack?

AUHack is Aarhus University's largest hackathon. Over a 36-hour period from April 8th to 10th, hackers from around the world will gather on campus to create and innovate their projects. This weekend gives you the opportunity to try out some of your craziest ideas, ideas you otherwise never would have got the time to complete. If you are up for it, this will also be a great opportunity to get to know new people and maybe even pair up and create fantastic hacks together.

FAQ

Should I come to this hackathon?

YES of course! If you are a student and above 18 years old. Students from all study programmes are welcome.

Is it for free?

Yes. Thanks to our sponsors we will provide free food, swag, snacks, drinks and a place for you to sleep. If you come from abroad we will furthermore try to help you with accommodation, transportation and maybe even some sightseeing advice.

How can I apply?

You must fill out the application form. After completing the form, you will after some time, receive an email that confirms your spot. Confirmation emails will be send on a rolling basis. The applications are open!

When is it?

AUHack will start in the evening of Friday, April 8th with the opening ceremony. There will be planned events Friday for those who arrive early. The closing ceremony will start Sunday, April 10th at noon. All work eligible for prizes must be completed between the opening and closing ceremony. Throughout the hackathon there will be hosted small events and talks. For a more detailed schedule see above.

Do I need to have a team?

No. You are more than welcome to make a team before the event, but loads of people come without a team. We will help everyone finding a team in the beginning of the event. Your team members can be from other universities around the world. One of the best things about hackathons is meeting hackers from all over the country.

I cannot code. Can I attend anyway?

You should definitely still attend, whether you have written lots of code or not even a single line. Other people have learned to code at hackathons, and we welcome you to try the same. In true hackathon spirit all students are very friendly and willing to help each other out.

Can I build on my hack before the event?

No. That’s unfair. You can come up with ideas, but not build them. You can of course use libraries, frameworks, and templates.

What can I win?

You have a fantastic opportunity to learn new stuff and make new friends, isn’t that just great? Everyone will be a winner!...
Okay, we will also have some kick-ass prices. They will be revealed later on.

I don’t know anyone else attending. Should I attend on my own?

Don’t let that stop you. A lot of other students will be coming on their own as well. We will have an amazing weekend! We will help everyone finding a team in the beginning of the event. Your team members can be from other universities around the world, and who doesn't love new friendships?

How do I get there?

After your registration is confirmed and you receive word that you’re coming to AUHack, we’ll share information about buses and possible travel reimbursements. More than likely, we’ll be sending a bus to a University near you.

What do I need to bring?

Need to have: Laptop and charger, cellphone and charger, sleeping bag, pillow, toiletries, a couple changes of clothes, and a well-rested and open mind.
Don’t need: a pre-determined team, and idea for an app, food or drink.
Absolutely no need to have: weapons, alcohol, illegal drugs, soldering equipment, animals, bad mood.

What if this FAQ didn't answer my questions?
